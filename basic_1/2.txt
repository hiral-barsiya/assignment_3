2. What is OOP? List OOP concepts


OOP:
-->OOP is a programming model in C++ that organizes programs around objects, rather than functions and logic.

All OOP Concepts:
-Encapsulation
-Abstraction
-Inheritance
-Polymorphism
-Composition
-class and objects
-constructor and destructor
-Method overloading and overriding
-Virtual functions
-Friend function and class
-Static members
-Namespace
